So Galbi Jjim

So galbi jjim is a traditional Korean braised beef short rib dish cooked with radish, chestnuts, jujubes, and soy sauce seasoning.

Ingredients

  • Beef short ribs
  • Radish
  • Chestnuts
  • Jujubes
  • Ginkgo nuts
  • Egg garnish
  • Soy sauce
  • Grated pear
  • Corn syrup
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger
  • Sesame oil
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the beef short ribs, soak in cold water, remove fat, and make cuts in the meat.
  2. Cut the radish into large cubes and peel the chestnuts.
  3. Remove the shells from the ginkgo nuts and stir-fry them in a pan.
  4. Grate the pear and strain it to make pear juice.
  5. Boil the ribs in water once and remove them.
  6. Make the seasoning sauce.
  7. Add the sauce and broth to the ribs, then add radish, chestnuts, jujubes, and ginkgo nuts and cook.
  8. Cook until done and garnish with egg garnish.
